- Custom instructions

- Speaking style

- Response timing patterns

Advanced Personal AI Features:
- Different behavior for family, friends, clients, and strangers
- Understand emotional connection with contacts
- Remember previous conversations

- Create a personal communication DNA profile
- Clone writing style ethically and transparently

Friend: "Kaha hai bhai?"

Family:
"Khane khaya?"
AI can reply in the natural style of the person.
